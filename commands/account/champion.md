### What to ask for — champion's call to action

At the end of their internal conversation, champion should ask:
> "Can I set up a call with [SC name] and [stakeholder name] on [date] to walk through
> [specific question they raised]? I want to make sure you have everything you need
> to feel comfortable."

Never ask "are you supportive?" — ask for a specific next step.

---

### Stay-in-touch cadence

| Touch | Frequency | Who | Purpose |
|-------|-----------|-----|---------|
| Check-in call | Weekly during active evaluation | SC + champion | Any new stakeholder concerns? |
| Deal status update | After every significant internal meeting | Champion → SC | What happened, what's the sentiment |
| Exec escalation | If economic buyer is going cold | AE + champion | Schedule exec briefing |

---

Confidence-tag all intelligence: 🟢 Confirmed / 🟡 Inferred / 🔴 Unknown.
Coach the champion: every 🔴 gap is something they need to find out internally.
