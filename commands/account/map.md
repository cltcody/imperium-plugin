Build a Mutual Action Plan for: **$ARGUMENTS**

A MAP is the joint commitment between SC/AE and the customer on what both sides will do
to reach a decision. It is built at the end of every significant call and sent within
the Golden Hours (immediately post-call). Never leave a call without a MAP.

Paste the meeting outcome, agreed next steps, and any timeline discussed.

### Gate question
Before closing this MAP, confirm: **"If we deliver all of our commitments by [date], will
you be in a position to make a decision by [target close date]?"**

If no → find out what's missing and add it to the plan.
If yes → document the commitment and send the MAP within 24 hours.
