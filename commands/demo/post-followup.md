Draft the post-demo follow-up email for: **$ARGUMENTS**

Paste context: account name, demo date, which modules you showed, what the customer reacted to (positively or with questions), and what was agreed as the next step.

The post-demo follow-up is sent within 24 hours. Its job is three things:
1. Show you were listening — recap what THEY responded to, not what you showed
2. Confirm the pains are real — "did we get it right?"
3. Lock in a specific next step — not "let's connect soon"

## SC checklist before sending

- [ ] Sent within 24 hours of the demo — not next week
- [ ] Subject line includes account name, demo focus, and next step reference
- [ ] The reactions column is specific to THIS demo — not generic "great session" language
- [ ] Open questions are named and owned — don't leave them floating
- [ ] Next step is a specific commitment — named action, named person, proposed date
- [ ] No "great to meet you" or "really enjoyed the conversation" filler
- [ ] If Salesforce is connected: update opportunity stage and log the demo activity
- [ ] Confidence-tag any value claims you reference (🟢 Confirmed / 🟡 Inferred)
