Draft a pre-demo invite email for: **$ARGUMENTS**

Paste context: account name, primary attendees, demo date/time, product focus, confirmed pains.

The pre-demo email is mandatory. It sets expectations, shows professionalism, and
increases demo attendance and engagement. Send 24-48 hours before the session.

---

## Pre-Demo Invite — [Account] | [Date]

**Subject:** [Account name] × ${user_config.company} — [Demo focus] — [Date + Time + Timezone]

Hi [Name],

Looking forward to our session on [date] at [time] [timezone].

**What we'll cover:**

In our last conversation, you mentioned [specific pain 1] and [specific pain 2].

you don't care about.

**Agenda ([total time]):**

| | Topic | Time |
|--|-------|------|

| 2 | [Pain 1 module name] | [X] min |
| 3 | [Pain 2 module name] | [X] min |

🔗 **Link:** [Meeting link]

Best,  
[SC name]

**SC checklist before sending:**
- [ ] Subject line includes account name, focus, and date/time
- [ ] Agenda items map directly to pains confirmed in discovery — not product features
- [ ] Recording notice included (respect privacy — always ask)
- [ ] Webcam request included
- [ ] Meeting link tested and working
- [ ] Sent 24-48 hours before the session (not same-day)
- [ ] Demo environment loaded and tested before sending this email
