### 3. Follow-Up Email (send within 2 hours)

**Subject:** [Account] × ${user_config.company} — [Date] conversation recap + next step

---

Hi [Name],

Thank you for the time today.

**What we covered:**
- [Key point 1 — use their exact words where possible]
- [Key point 2]
- [Key point 3 if relevant]

**My read on your priorities:**
[1–2 sentences summarising the pains you heard. This is a soft confirmation — if you got it wrong, they'll tell you now rather than at the demo.]

**Agreed next step:**
[Specific — what happens, who does it, proposed date. Not "we'll be in touch."]

I'll send a calendar invite shortly.

[Your name]
