# Standard Questions — All Suites

These questions apply to every discovery engagement regardless of suite.
They cover the universal context needed to scope any [COMPANY] solution.

Generate adapted versions of each using account research from Step 2.
Where research provides a hypothesis, pre-fill as: *"Our understanding: [X] — please confirm."*
Questions already answered from research should be marked 🟡 Confirm (not asked fresh).

---

## 01 — Company Profile

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 1.1 | What is your primary industry and the main products / goods you handle? | Drives module relevance (food, pharma, industrial, etc.) |
| 1.2 | What is your approximate annual revenue and employee count? | Sizes the implementation and shapes the ROI model |
| 1.3 | Which geographies do you operate in — and which are in scope for this project? | Determines country-specific compliance and language needs |
| 1.4 | How many legal entities / operating companies are in scope? | Critical for multi-tenant or multi-org deployments |
| 1.5 | Do you have any recent or planned M&A, divestitures, or reorgs that affect this project? | Flags scope risk and potential timeline disruption |

## 02 — Technology Landscape

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 2.1 | What is your primary ERP system and version? (SAP, Oracle, JDE, Dynamics, other) | Drives integration approach and add-on vs. standalone |
| 2.2 | Are there additional systems relevant to this project? (WMS, OMS, PLM, webshop, MDM) | Uncovers integration complexity early |
| 2.3 | Do you use a middleware or integration platform? (MuleSoft, Boomi, TIBCO, Azure, other) | Shapes the technical architecture for integrations |
| 2.4 | What is your preference for integration type? (API, XML/EDI, Add-On, flat file) | Aligns with IT capability and existing patterns |
| 2.5 | Is IT managed internally or via a third party? | Determines availability and skills for implementation |
| 2.6 | Do you require Single Sign-On (SSO / SAML)? | Common late-stage blocker if missed early |
| 2.7 | What are your data residency or sovereignty requirements? (US only, EU, APAC, etc.) | Critical for cloud/hosting decisions |
| 2.8 | Any security or compliance certifications required? (SOC 2, ISO 27001, FedRAMP) | Affects vendor qualification |

## 03 — Volumes & Scale

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 3.1 | How many transactions / records per year are in scope? (shipments, orders, entries, etc.) | Sizes the platform and licensing |
| 3.2 | How many users will need access? Break out by role if possible. | Shapes the user licence model |
| 3.3 | How many trading partners / counterparties are in your master data? | Relevant to screening, [PRODUCT_D], supplier portals |
| 3.4 | How many products / SKUs are in scope? | Drives classification volume and data migration effort |

## 04 — Project Context

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 4.1 | What is the primary driver for this evaluation? (replace legacy, compliance gap, cost reduction, growth, audit) | Defines the CBI and urgency |
| 4.2 | Is there a specific deadline or business event driving the timeline? | Reveals compelling event |
| 4.3 | What is your target go-live date? | Scopes the implementation plan |
| 4.4 | Is budget approved, in process, or not yet allocated? | Qualifies the deal stage |
| 4.5 | What does the evaluation and decision process look like? Who is involved? | Maps the decision process for [QUALIFICATION_FRAMEWORK] |
| 4.6 | Are you evaluating other vendors? If so, who? | Competitive landscape |
| 4.7 | Have you attempted to solve this problem before? What happened? | Prevents repeating a failed approach |

## 05 — Value & ROI Baseline

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 5.1 | How many FTEs are currently dedicated to this process? Where are they located? | Baseline for headcount ROI calculation |
| 5.2 | What does this process cost you today — in time, errors, penalties, or manual effort? | Seeds the business case |
| 5.3 | What does a failure in this process cost — one incident, one audit, one delayed shipment? | Quantifies the risk side of the business case |
| 5.4 | What is the expected payback period or ROI target for this investment? | Sets the financial hurdle rate |

## 06 — External Parties

| # | Question | Why it matters |
|---|----------|----------------|
| 6.1 | Do you use logistics service providers (LSPs), freight forwarders, or customs brokers? Which ones? | Connectivity to carrier/broker networks |
| 6.2 | Will external parties need access to the solution (brokers, suppliers, customers)? | Drives portal and access management requirements |
| 6.3 | What type of electronic communication do you do today with trading partners? (EDI, API, portal) | Baseline for partner integration scope |
